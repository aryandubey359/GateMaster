Welcome to our Website

To all the students who could not go to any Gate Coaching Academies due to prevailing Covid Conditions or could not afford those highly expensive coaching classes, you have come to the right place. Our website is totally free and has one of the most top notch content you can find on the Internet regarding Gate Examination.

GateMaster is a website to help the students appearing for Gate examination in the near future. It contains video lectures on different topics and notes on the same for better preparation of the students. It also contain previous year Question Papers, both with solutions and without solutions so that the students can practice thoroughly. Anyone who has just started preparing and has little to no knowledge regarding the examination, can educate himself in the About Gate section where you can get previous year cutoffs, important dates of this year examination, application fee and much more.

By preparing from our website and scoring a good GATE score, you can get in your dream college for masters degree and even apply for jobs in the Public Sector.
